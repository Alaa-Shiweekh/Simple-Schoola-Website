Who We Are

A smarter way to manage your academy

Schoola Academy provides educators and administrators a centralized platform to manage student records efficiently. Add students, assign departments, record grades, and track performance — with full search, filter, and sorting capabilities.

Built with simplicity at its core, Schoola is accessible from any device, letting you manage your institution wherever you are.

Student Registration

Add new students with name, age, gender, department, and grade in a streamlined form with instant validation.
